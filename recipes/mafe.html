Mafe recipe

Description

This also is a dish from westAfrica and its origin is fairly discussed as every country jokingly claims it belonging to them

Engredients

  • peanut butter
  • oil
  • rice
  • meat
  • vegetables
  • gombo pouder
  • tomato sauce

Steps

  1. you start by frieing the meat and add a little of tomato sauce after 6-7mins
  2. add some water 5mins after
  3. add the peanut better after you watered it to make sure it get mixed and mix it
  4. spice the sauce with your favorite spices and add the vegetables
  5. add the gombo pouder and mix it
  6. vaporize the rice
  7. Once it gets ready set the table and enjoy!!!
